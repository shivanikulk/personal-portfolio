Interactive Prototype

Prototyping Interactive Puzzle

This was a small exploration from the Museum Experience Project. The idea was to create rapid Most Viable Prototype that users can interact with to test the working of the game mechanics.

What was built

01

The prototype is part of an app experience built in plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It uses a 3x3 image-based tile grid, a swap interaction model, a limited-hearts constraint, a temporary hint overlay, score feedback, and two end states for winning and losing with an option to replay.
